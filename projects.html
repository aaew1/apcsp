AP Literature Paradise Lost Paper




This was a paper I wrote for AP Literature on a complicated issue presented in the epic poem "Paradise Lost" by John Milton. The issue I chose was the portrayal of Satan as a hero. Usually Satan is considered evil however in Pradise Lost, Milton puts Satan in the role of a traditional epic hero. However, Satan still does horrible things such as dooming humanity so that position presents a knotty problem. In the essay I presented all the nuances of this problem. I enjoyed writing this and I learned a lot about epic poetry and the classical era of literature.
